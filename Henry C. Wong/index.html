  • My History With Gaming

    My first experience with video games was when I was 3 years old. My dad had picked up one of those shady copies that have “over 99 games” on the cd for free at a Chinese New Year festival. My dad picked me up and sat me down on his lap in front of our computer running on Windows 98 (2000 hadn’t come out yet). The only game he recognized and picked to play was Doom II, not the most kid friendly game. Although my dad probably didn’t care much for the moment, this moment would peak my interest in computer programming.
